Token Taxonomy Artifact

Roles Behavior

A token can have behaviors that the class will restrict invocations to a select set of parties or accounts that are members of a role or group. This is a generic behavior that can apply to a token many times to represent many role definitions within the template.

Behavior Invocations:

GetRoleMembersRequestA request to view the members of a particular role.GetRoleMembersResponseIncludes a list of parties or accounts that are members of the role.
AddRoleMemberRequestAdd a party or account to a particular role.AddRoleMemberResponseA confirmation receipt or error may be returned to the invoker based on the outcome of the request.
RemoveRoleMemberRequestRemove a party or account to a particular roleRemoveRoleMemberResponseA confirmation receipt or error may be returned to the invoker based on the outcome of the request.
